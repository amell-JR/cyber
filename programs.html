Academic Programs

Comprehensive cybersecurity education from undergraduate to doctoral levels

World-Class Cybersecurity Education

Our comprehensive academic programs are designed to prepare students for successful careers in cybersecurity, combining theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on experience.

Undergraduate Programs

B.Sc. Computer Science (Cybersecurity)

4 Years

A comprehensive undergraduate program that provides students with a solid foundation in computer science principles while specializing in cybersecurity concepts and practices.

Core Areas of Study:
  • Network Security
  • Cryptography
  • Digital Forensics
  • Ethical Hacking
  • Risk Management
  • Security Policies
  • Incident Response
  • Secure Programming

Duration: 4 Years

Credits: 120 Credit Hours

Entry Requirements: JAMB/UTME

Career Prospects: Security Analyst, Consultant

B.Sc. Information Technology (Security)

4 Years

Focused on the practical application of information technology with emphasis on security implementation, system administration, and cybersecurity operations.

Core Areas of Study:
  • System Administration
  • Cloud Security
  • Mobile Security
  • IoT Security
  • Database Security
  • Web Security
  • Security Operations
  • Compliance Management

Duration: 4 Years

Credits: 120 Credit Hours

Entry Requirements: JAMB/UTME

Career Prospects: IT Security Specialist

Postgraduate Programs

M.Sc. Cybersecurity

2 Years

Advanced master's program focusing on cutting-edge cybersecurity research, advanced threat analysis, and security architecture design.

Specialization Areas:
  • Advanced Threat Analysis
  • Security Architecture
  • Cyber Threat Intelligence
  • Digital Forensics
  • Cryptographic Systems

Duration: 2 Years (Full-time)

Credits: 36 Credit Hours

Thesis: Required

M.Sc. Digital Forensics

2 Years

Specialized program in digital investigation techniques, evidence collection, and forensic analysis for cybercrime investigation.

Specialization Areas:
  • Computer Forensics
  • Mobile Device Forensics
  • Network Forensics
  • Malware Analysis
  • Legal Aspects

Duration: 2 Years (Full-time)

Credits: 36 Credit Hours

Thesis: Required

M.Sc. Network Security

Advanced study of network security protocols, infrastructure protection, and secure network design for enterprise environments.

Specialization Areas:

  • Wireless Security

  • Firewall Technologies
  • VPN Technologies

Duration: 2 Years (Full-time)

Credits: 36 Credit Hours

Thesis: Required

Doctoral Programs

Ph.D. in Cybersecurity

3-5 Years

Advanced research-focused doctoral program for students interested in contributing to the cybersecurity field through original research and innovation.

Research Areas:
  • AI Security
  • Blockchain Security
  • IoT Security
  • Quantum Cryptography
  • Privacy Engineering
  • Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Human Factors
  • Security Economics

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate

  • JAMB UTME with minimum of 200
  • 5 O'Level credits including Mathematics and English
  • Physics and Chemistry required
  • Post-UTME screening
  • Medical fitness certificate

Master's

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum CGPA of 3.0/5.0 or 2.5/4.0
  • Two academic references
  • Statement of purpose
  • English proficiency (if applicable)

Doctoral

  • Master's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum CGPA of 3.5/5.0 or 3.0/4.0
  • Research proposal
  • Three academic references
  • Interview with faculty committee
