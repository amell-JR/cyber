About Our Department

Pioneering cybersecurity education and research in Nigeria

Our Mission

To provide world-class cybersecurity education, conduct cutting-edge research, and develop skilled professionals who will protect Nigeria's digital infrastructure and contribute to global cybersecurity initiatives.

We are committed to fostering innovation, ethical practices, and excellence in cybersecurity education while building strong partnerships with industry and government agencies.

Our Vision

To be the leading cybersecurity department in West Africa, recognized globally for excellence in education, research, and innovation in digital security.

We envision a future where our graduates are at the forefront of cybersecurity leadership, protecting critical infrastructure and advancing the field through groundbreaking research and ethical practices.

Department Overview

The Cybersecurity Department at the University of Uyo was established in 2018 as part of the Computer Science Faculty, recognizing the critical need for specialized cybersecurity education in Nigeria.

Our department combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, ensuring our students are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern digital landscape. We offer comprehensive programs from undergraduate to doctoral levels, all designed to meet international standards and industry requirements.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry partnerships, we provide an environment where students can develop the skills, knowledge, and ethical foundation necessary to become leaders in cybersecurity.

Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education and research
  • Integrity: Maintaining ethical practices in all our activities
  • Innovation: Embracing new technologies and methodologies
  • Collaboration: Building partnerships for mutual growth
  • Service: Contributing to national and global cybersecurity
  • Diversity: Embracing inclusive learning environments

Our Journey

2018 - Department Establishment

The Cybersecurity Department was officially established as part of the Computer Science Faculty, with initial enrollment of 50 students.

2019 - First Research Lab

Opened our first dedicated cybersecurity research laboratory with state-of-the-art equipment and software tools.

2020 - Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with leading cybersecurity firms and government agencies for internships and research collaboration.

2021 - First Graduates

Celebrated our first graduating class of 45 students, with 95% employment rate within 6 months of graduation.
