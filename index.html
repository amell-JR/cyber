Welcome to UNIUYO Cybersecurity Department

As part of the University of Uyo's commitment to academic excellence, our Cybersecurity Department embodies the university's vision of utilizing available human and technological resources for teaching, research, community service, and sustainable development.

Following UNIUYO's motto of "Thought & Service," we are dedicated to developing cybersecurity professionals who will serve Nigeria and the global community. Our department contributes to the university's legacy of over 100 inaugural lectures and continuous academic innovation, preparing students to address the evolving challenges of digital security in our interconnected world.
